Best Pizza in Town

Come to our Ontario pizzeria for classic thin-crust New York-style, deep-dish Chicago-style, or tangy BBQ pizzas with meats and veggies. Dine-in or takeout. Experience the best pizza in town!

Our selection of pizza.

New York-style pizza: a thin-crust pizza with a crispy exterior and chewy interior, tangy sauce, and a special blend of melted cheeses. Served in large triangular slices, it's an iconic and delicious food of the city.

Chicago-style pizza: deep-dish with thick, buttery crust, piled high with toppings such as Italian sausage, pepperoni, onions, peppers, and mushrooms. Rich sauce and cheese blend for a hearty and indulgent meal, perfect for sharing. An iconic dish of Chicago cuisine.

BBQ pizza: tangy BBQ sauce base, topped with meats like chicken, pulled pork or beef, red onions, peppers, and melted cheese. Smoky flavor, crispy or chewy crust. Perfect for summer barbecues or cozy nights in, loved by all.
